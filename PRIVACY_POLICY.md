# Privacy Policy for AfroChinaTrade

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

## Introduction

AfroChinaTrade ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and password
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture, business details, and preferences
- **Contact Information:** Delivery addresses, billing information
- **Identity Verification:** Documents for supplier verification (when applicable)

### Transaction Information
- **Order Data:** Purchase history, cart contents, payment methods
- **Financial Information:** Payment details (processed securely through third-party providers)
- **Refund Requests:** Transaction disputes and refund information

### Usage Information
- **App Activity:** Pages visited, products viewed, search queries
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data:** Approximate location for delivery and regional content (with permission)
- **Communication Data:** Messages, reviews, support tickets

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, access times
- **Analytics Data:** App performance, crash reports, usage patterns
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Session data, preferences, authentication tokens

- **Service Delivery:** Process orders, manage accounts, provide customer support

- **Security:** Prevent fraud, protect against unauthorized access
